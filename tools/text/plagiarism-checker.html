About Plagiarism
What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of using someone else's work or ideas without proper attribution. It's considered academic dishonesty and copyright infringement.

  • Direct copying without credit
  • Paraphrasing without attribution
  • Self-plagiarism
  • Accidental plagiarism
How to Avoid Plagiarism
  • Always cite your sources
  • Use quotation marks for direct quotes
  • Paraphrase properly
  • Use plagiarism checkers
  • Keep detailed research notes
