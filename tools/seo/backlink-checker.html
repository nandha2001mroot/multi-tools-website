Backlink Quality Indicators
High-Quality Backlinks:
  • From authoritative, relevant websites
  • DoFollow links (pass SEO value)
  • Natural anchor text
  • Contextually relevant
  • Good domain authority
Low-Quality Backlinks:
  • From spammy websites
  • Link farms or PBNs
  • Exact match anchor text
  • Low domain authority
  • Irrelevant content
