About Domain Authority
What is Domain Authority?

Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how well a website will rank on search engine result pages (SERPs).

  • Scored on a 1-100 scale
  • Higher scores indicate stronger SEO
  • Based on multiple factors
  • Updated monthly
Interpreting Scores
  • 1-20: New websites
  • 21-30: Low authority
  • 31-40: Below average
  • 41-50: Average
  • 51-60: Above average
  • 61-70: Good
  • 71-80: Very good
  • 81-100: Excellent
