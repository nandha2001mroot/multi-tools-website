Tip Guidelines
Standard Tip Percentages:
  • 10%: Poor service
  • 15%: Average service
  • 18-20%: Good service
  • 20-25%: Excellent service
  • 25%+: Outstanding service
When to Tip More:
  • Exceptional service quality
  • Special requests fulfilled
  • Large parties or special occasions
  • Fine dining establishments
  • Personalized attention
Pro Tips:
  • Round up small amounts for convenience
  • Consider the total experience, not just service
  • Tip based on pre-tax amount in most cases
  • Split evenly unless someone ordered significantly more
