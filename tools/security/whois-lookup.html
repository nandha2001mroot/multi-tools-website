Privacy Protection
Domain Privacy:

Many registrars offer privacy protection services that hide personal information from public Whois records.

  • Protects against spam
  • Prevents identity theft
  • Reduces unwanted calls
  • Legal compliance
GDPR Impact:

The General Data Protection Regulation has limited the amount of personal information displayed in Whois records.

  • Reduced personal data exposure
  • Enhanced privacy rights
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Balanced access needs
