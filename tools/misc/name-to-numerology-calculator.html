About Numerology
Pythagorean System:
  • Uses numbers 1-9
  • Based on Greek mathematics
  • Most common system
  • Easy to calculate
Chaldean System:
  • Uses numbers 1-8
  • More complex calculations
  • Ancient Babylonian origin
  • Considers sound vibrations
Important Note:
